How the system works

  • air quality is monitored from Air Tracker devices spread across urban, industrial and rural areas
  • air quality data from Air Tracker devices is streamed to cloud
  • users can access data from web portal in real-time
  • web portal will enable mapping of air quality information>

System specifications

  • Solar powered
  • multi-sensored to identify pollution in a range of different contexts, including urban and industrial areas, and mining
  • can be installed from a long distance from the base module

Gas sensors to cover most dangerous air pollutants in urban and industrial areas, including in mining affected areas:

  • Methane
  • Ammonia
  • - Nitrogen dioxide
  • Hydrocarbons
  • Sulphur Oxide
  • Formaldehyde
  • Benzene
  • Ethylbenzene
  • Toluene
  • Xylene
