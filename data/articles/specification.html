How the system works

  • air quality is monitored from “MushroomAir” devices spread across urban, industrial and rural areas
  • air quality data from “MushroomAir” devices is streamed to cloud
  • users can access data from web portal in real-time
  • web portal will enable mapping of air quality information>

System specifications

  • Solar powered
  • multi-sensored to identify pollution in a range of different contexts, including urban and industrial areas, and mining
  • can be installed from a long distance from the base module
