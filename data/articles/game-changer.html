Air-Tracker wireless air quality monitor

Low maintenance, continuous outdoor weather resilient

Air-Tracker is a continuous, wireless outdoor air quality sensor with gps. Air-Tracker utilises technology that detects volatile organic compounds down to low parts-per-billion levels.

Its wireless capabilities allow a network of virtually unlimited number of instruments to be deployed around any location with data downloaded and managed independently by each unit.

Air-Tracker continuously monitors and logs the data it gathers into the cloud. The data is high-quality, real time and readily available for detailed analysis

The accompanied interactive software shows real trend data captured within each instrument or each location or site. In this way the need for frequent site visits is eliminated thus saving both time and money.
